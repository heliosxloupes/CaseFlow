CaseArc Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 2, 2026

CaseArc ("we", "our", or "us") is a surgical case logging tool designed for medical residents. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

  • Voice recordings: Processed in real-time for transcription only. Not stored on our servers.
  • Case log data: Procedure descriptions, dates, attending names, and CPT codes you submit. This data is transmitted to the ACGME ADS portal on your behalf.
  • Account credentials: Your ACGME username and password are encrypted at rest using AES-256 and never logged or shared.
  • Usage logs: Anonymous error and diagnostic logs for app stability. No personally identifiable information.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

  • Patient names, dates of birth, MRNs, or any Protected Health Information (PHI)
  • Location data
  • Contacts or calendar data
  • Biometric data

3. Microphone

CaseArc requests microphone access solely to enable voice dictation for case logging. Audio is transcribed locally or via a secure API call and is not stored.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Case data is transmitted only to the ACGME ADS portal using your credentials. We use Anthropic's Claude API for AI transcription and code lookup — see Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention

Case submission history is retained on our servers for up to 12 months to enable the history and milestones features. You can request deletion at any time by contacting us.

6. Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. ACGME credentials are encrypted with AES-256. We do not store voice recordings.

7. Children

CaseArc is designed for use by medical professionals (18+). We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

8. Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests: privacy@caseflow.app

9. Changes

We may update this policy. The effective date above will reflect any changes.
