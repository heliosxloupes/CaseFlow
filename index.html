CaseArc Terms of Service

CaseArc is a limited beta tool for assisting surgical residents with case capture, review, and ACGME submission workflows. It is provided for internal testing and evaluation only.

Use of the beta

  • You are responsible for verifying all case data before submission to ACGME.
  • You agree not to share your beta key or allow anyone else to use your account.
  • CaseArc may log errors, submission failures, and beta feedback tied to your account for debugging and support.

Privacy and security

  • CaseArc stores account and beta usage data needed to operate the service.
  • ACGME credential workflows and beta diagnostics may include operational metadata for troubleshooting.
  • Do not enter protected information that your institution does not permit in this beta environment.

No warranty

This beta is provided as-is for testing. You should independently confirm any output, submission status, or educational tracking information before relying on it.
